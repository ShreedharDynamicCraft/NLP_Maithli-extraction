About me

I'm a passionate advocate for open knowledge and technologies with a strong background in Human resource management and Program management, personally I am a budding social worker, an average IT developer (with AI support), ex NIIT answer sheet checker, failed press reporter, Civil Engineer, almost-kicked-out-of-college graduate, Master in Business Administration. Through various endeavors, both successful and unsuccessful, I've cultivated a broad range of abilities. I have been actively involved as a volunteer with several organizations, including Telugu Wikipedia (since 2006), eTelugu.org, FSF.org, tmad.org, Satya Sai Seva Samiti,NHRDN and Hyderabad Management Association.
