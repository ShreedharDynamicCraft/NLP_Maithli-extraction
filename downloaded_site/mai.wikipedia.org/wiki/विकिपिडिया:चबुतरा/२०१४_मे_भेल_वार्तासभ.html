स्वागत

[सम्पादन करी]

मैथिली विकिपिडिया में अहाँ सभ के स्वागत अछि । ऐ ठाम अहाँ कोनो विषय सँ सम्बन्धित प्रश्न पुइछ सकएत छी आ अपन विचार सेहो व्यक्त कऽ सकएत छी । मैथिली विकिपिडिया अखन सुरुवात अवस्थामें रहल अछि मुदा अहाँ सभ लोकनि सँ हार्दिक अनुरोध अछि जे ऐ विकिमें अपन योगदान दएल जाए आ एकरा आन विकि जोंका व्यवस्थित आ बढिया बनाओल जाए । अहि दिसामें किछु मित्र लोकनि सभ लागल सहो देखलौ य । सम्पादन करु आ ई विकि के आगा ल जाबए मे मद्दत करु । धन्यवाद बिप्लब आनन्द (वार्ता) १५:३३, २६ नवंबर २०१४ (NPT)

Standard Bot Policy

[सम्पादन करी]

Sorry for English. Please translate this into Maithili as needed!

Hello,

Meta maintains a Standard Bot Policy that allows global bots (these bots are allowed to fix double redirects) ans automatic approval (allows Stewards to directly flag for double-redirect fixing bot if unopposed for a week by community). I would like to ask Maithili Wikipedia to enable this; so that you guys does have to worry about double redirects. (PS. Global bot allows interwiki fixing, but this has been migrated into Wikidata before the creation of the Maithili Wikipedia.) Thanks for your time, and if you have question, feel free to ask me on Meta user talk page.

(PS 2: I added magic word __NEWSECTIONLINK__ which givies new section button on the top.)

Invitation to Bengali Wikipedia 10th Anniversary Celebration Conference 2015

[सम्पादन करी]

Hi Community members,

Bengali Wikipedia community is organizing its 10th Anniversary Celebration Conference at Kolkata on 9 & 10 January 2015.
You can see our Official event page and the Facebook event page.

We are planning to invite our friends and well-wishers from different language wiki communities in India to this most auspicious occasion hosted by Bengali Wikimedia community! We are also planning to arrange few scholarships for non-Bengali Indic Wikimedians who are interested in participating in this event. Please select your Five (5) scholarship [1] delegates from your community member for this conference and announce it here before 10th December 2014.

  • To participate in Conference, please register here
  • To participate in Conference as a speaker please submit your paper here.
  • To participate Photography Contest , click here


We look forward to see you at Kolkata on 9 & 10 January 2015

1) Scholarship included with Travel reimbursement upto 2000/- + dormitory or shared accommodation + meals during the conference hours

On behalf of Bengali Wikipedia Community (Sorry for writing in English)

[सम्पादन करी]

दिसम्बर, २०१४ के मुख्य लेखके लेल आवेदक बिप्लब आनन्द सँ विद्यापति लेख मुख्य लेखके आवेदन प्राप्त भेल मुख्य लेख समन्वय समिति सँ इ लेख मुख्य लेखका मापदण्ड अनुसार भेल नै भेलपूनरावलोकन कएर मतदानके लेल सिफारिस भेला के कारण, विकिपिडिया:मुख्य लेख/आवेदनसभ/विद्यापति में अपन मत राखि निर्णयमें सहयोग करए के लेल अनुरोध करए छी।
