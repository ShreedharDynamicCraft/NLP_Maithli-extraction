This representation of the 
National Coat of Arms
 of 
Singapore
 is based solely on its 
blazon
 as set out in the Part 4 of the First Schedule of the 
National Symbols Act 2022
. Therefore, it differs from the official representation of the Coat of Arms which also appears in the Rules. The copyright in the official representation is held by the 
Government of Singapore
, and will not expire till 1 January 2030. Therefore, reproductions of the official version of the Coat of Arms cannot be uploaded to the Wikimedia Commons until that date.


To avoid confusion when using this representation of the Coat of Arms, it is suggested that you should mention in the image caption that it is a version of the Coat of Arms constructed on the basis of its blazon.

Català: 
 
De gules, un creixent d'argent sobremuntat per cinc estrelles de cinc puntes d'argent. Té com a suports, un lleó d'or a la destra i un tigre a la sinistra. El lema és: Majulah Singapura.
English: 
 
The National Coat of Arms of Singapore or State Crest consists of a shield on which is emblazoned a white crescent moon and 5 white stars against a red background. Supporting the shield shall be a lion on the left and a tiger on the right. Below the shield shall be a banner with the words "Majulah Singapura" inscribed on it.
Español: 
 
De gules, un creciente de plata surmontado de cinco estrellas de cinco puntas de plata. Tiene como soportes, un león de oro a la diestra y un tigre a la siniestra. El lema es: Majulah Singapura.
