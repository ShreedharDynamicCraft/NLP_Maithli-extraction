Use to direct people to a discussion elsewhere.

Examples:

{{
Discussing 
|talk=Umlauts}}
 to link to discussion on this talk page or the talk page of this page:


 
वार्तालाप
 चरणमे
...


{{
Discussing 
|talkpage=Wikipedia talk:Manual of Style|talk=Umlauts}}
 to link to discussion at another page:


 
वार्तालाप
 चरणमे
...


{{
Discussing 
|talkpage=Wikipedia talk:Manual of Style#Umlauts}}
 to link to discussion at another page, more concisely:


 
वार्तालाप
 चरणमे
...


{{
Discussing 
|talkpage=Wikipedia talk:Manual of Style#Umlauts|1=Participants here may be interested in this MOS debate.}}
 to add a note:


 
वार्तालाप
 चरणमे
: Participants here may be interested in this MOS debate.
