 &

Pratapgarh district, Rajasthan
 & 
Pratapgarh district, Uttar Pradesh


Balrampur district, Chhattisgarh
 & 
Balrampur district, Uttar Pradesh
.

 shares a name with

 shares a name with

 shares a name with

 shares a name with

,

 shares a name with

, shares a name with

one

, so since then the two parts have been administered separately)

, with
